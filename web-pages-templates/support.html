Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with ProTech?
After downloading ProTech from the Mac App Store:
  1. Open the app and create your admin account
  2. Set up your business information in Settings
  3. Add your first customer
  4. Create your first ticket
Check out our in-app tutorials for guided walkthroughs!
How do subscriptions work?
ProTech offers two subscription options:
  • Monthly: Billed monthly, cancel anytime
  • Annual: Billed yearly, save ~30%
All subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID. To cancel, go to your App Store account settings â†’ Subscriptions â†’ ProTech â†’ Cancel Subscription.
How do I backup my data?
ProTech offers multiple backup options:
  1. Cloud Sync: Enable in Settings â†’ Cloud Sync (recommended)
  2. Local Backup: Your data is automatically stored on your Mac
  3. Export: Export your data as CSV or JSON from Settings â†’ Data Management
We recommend enabling cloud sync for automatic backups!
Can I integrate with Square?
Yes! ProTech integrates with Square for:
  • Payment processing
  • Customer synchronization
  • Inventory management
To connect: Go to Settings â†’ Integrations â†’ Square and enter your Square credentials.
How do I send SMS notifications to customers?
ProTech uses Twilio for SMS messaging:
  1. Create a Twilio account at twilio.com
  2. Get a phone number from Twilio
  3. In ProTech, go to Settings â†’ Communications â†’ SMS
  4. Enter your Twilio credentials
  5. Configure automated notifications
Note: Twilio charges apply for SMS messages (typically $0.0075 per SMS in the US).
How do I generate invoices and estimates?
For Invoices:
  1. Open a ticket
  2. Click "Generate Invoice"
  3. Add line items (parts, labor, etc.)
  4. Save and send to customer
For Estimates:
  1. Go to Estimates â†’ New Estimate
  2. Select customer and add line items
  3. Set expiration date
  4. Send to customer for approval
Can I use ProTech on multiple Macs?
Yes! With cloud sync enabled:
  1. Install ProTech on each Mac
  2. Sign in with the same account
  3. Enable cloud sync in Settings
  4. Your data will automatically sync across all devices
Your subscription covers all your Macs.
What if I need to refund a customer?
To process a refund:
  1. Go to the payment record
  2. Click "Refund"
  3. Enter refund amount
  4. Add reason/notes
  5. Confirm refund
If paid through Square, the refund is processed automatically. Otherwise, record as a manual refund.
How do I manage employee permissions?
ProTech has role-based permissions:
  1. Go to Settings â†’ Employees
  2. Add new employee or edit existing
  3. Set role: Admin, Technician, or Counter Staff
  4. Admins have full access
  5. Technicians can manage tickets but not financials
  6. Counter Staff can check-in customers and view tickets

  2. Include:
