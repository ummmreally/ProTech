Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 13, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to ProTech ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our ProTech application ("App").

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

We collect information you directly provide when using the App:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, business name, phone number
  • Business Data: Customer information, repair tickets, invoices, payments, inventory
  • Device Information: Device models, serial numbers, repair details
  • Communication Data: SMS messages, email communications (when you enable these features)

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: Features used, actions performed, timestamps
  • Device Information: macOS version, device model, app version
  • Crash Reports: Error logs and diagnostics (if you opt in)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the App's functionality
  • Process your subscription and payments
  • Sync your data across devices (if enabled)
  • Send service-related notifications
  • Improve and optimize the App
  • Provide customer support
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

Your business data is primarily stored locally on your Mac using Core Data. This data remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers unless you enable cloud sync features.

4.2 Cloud Storage (Optional)

If you enable cloud sync:

  • Data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Stored on Supabase servers (https://supabase.com)
  • Subject to Supabase's security measures and privacy policy
  • You can disable cloud sync at any time

4.3 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encryption of sensitive data
  • Secure API communications (HTTPS)
  • Regular security updates
  • Access controls and authentication

5. Third-Party Services

The App integrates with third-party services (only if you enable them):

5.1 Supabase (Cloud Sync)

For optional cloud storage and synchronization. Privacy Policy: https://supabase.com/privacy

5.2 Square (Payment Processing)

For payment processing and customer sync (if configured). Privacy Policy: https://squareup.com/us/en/legal/general/privacy

5.3 Twilio (SMS Notifications)

For sending SMS messages to customers (if configured). Privacy Policy: https://www.twilio.com/legal/privacy

5.4 Apple In-App Purchases

For subscription management. Subject to Apple's Privacy Policy: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

6. Your Customer Data

Important: Data about your customers (names, contact info, devices, etc.) belongs to you. We do not access, sell, or share your customer data. You are responsible for:

  • Obtaining necessary consents from your customers
  • Complying with applicable data protection laws (GDPR, CCPA, etc.)
  • Managing your customers' data privacy rights

7. Data Retention

We retain your information:

  • Account Data: Until you delete your account
  • Business Data: Stored locally on your device; you control deletion
  • Cloud Data: Until you delete it or disable cloud sync
  • Subscription Data: As required by Apple and tax laws

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Correction: Update or correct your information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Export: Export your data in a portable format
  • Opt-Out: Disable optional features (cloud sync, analytics)

9. Children's Privacy

ProTech is not intended for use by individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. We will notify you of changes by:

  • Updating the "Last updated" date
  • Posting a notice in the App
  • Sending an email notification (for significant changes)

11. International Users

ProTech is available worldwide. If you use the App from outside the United States, your data may be transferred to and stored in the United States or other countries. By using the App, you consent to such transfers.

12. California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Know what personal information we collect
  • Know whether we sell or share your information (we don't)
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Opt-out of the sale of your information (not applicable)
  • Non-discrimination for exercising your rights
